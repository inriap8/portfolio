Headline-Based Indonesian Political Misinformation Classification: A Comparative Study of Naive Bayes, SVM, CNN, and IndoBERT Models

Journal of Applied Informatics and Computing (JAIC) · e-ISSN 2548-6861 · 4th of 6 authors

Classified 5,132 Indonesian political news headlines as misinformation or valid, based solely on headlines. IndoBERT achieved the best results (90.25% accuracy, 90.24% Macro F1), though McNemar's test showed differences between models were not statistically significant.
